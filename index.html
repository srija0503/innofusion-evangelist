Evangelist selection process

  • Applicants must fill out the form by clicking on the above link.
  • Our team will review your application.
  • If your application seems to be a good fit, you'll receive a call / message from our team for a short telephonic interview.
  • Final shortlisted candidates will be selected as evangelists.

After you get selected ?

  • Selected evangelists will be added in the official WhatsApp group of InnoFusion 3.0 hackathon evangelists.
  • Every evangelist will be provided with unique referral code.
  • All other details will be communicated through WhatsApp.

Responsibilities
